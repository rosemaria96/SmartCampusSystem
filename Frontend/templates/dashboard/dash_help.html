Frequently Asked Questions

To apply, visit the Academics page, select your desired course, and click on "View Details" to access the application form. You can also visit the Admissions office on campus.

The central library is open from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM on weekdays and 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM on weekends. During exam periods, it remains open 24/7.
